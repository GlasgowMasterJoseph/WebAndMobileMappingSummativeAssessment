About This Map

This map was designed for the Web & Mobile Mapping course at the University of Glasgow to allow you to view the weather information at airports across Florida through the OpenMeteo API. The map was designed in Mapbox and the dataset used can be found here.

Instructions

Click on an airport to view detailed weather information, including temperature, wind speed and weather conditions.

You can make use of the right-hand side navigation controls: search, geolocate, zoom and pitch. You can also switch between monochrome and satellite map views and enable fullscreen. The scale bar is in the bottom left.
